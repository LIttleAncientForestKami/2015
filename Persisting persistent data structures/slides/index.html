Your Kung Fu is weak, old man!

  • Sample "business" domain
  • Let's see how it works

Choose players (and their decks)

  • Each player has their own deck of 20 cards
  • Each deck represents a particular martial arts style

Special Phase: Initiative

  • All players draw 4 cards into their hand
  • All players place one "basic" card face down
  • Lowest card goes first, if same redraw to 4 and repeat

Main Game

  • Each player takes a turn
  • A turn has 2 optional phases: play an attack, put a card in stance

Play an attack

  • Play one of more cards to form a valid attack
  • At most one card may come from your hand, others must be from stance
  • Targeted player may defend (with a defend card) or counter with a trump attack
  • Suits "trump" each other in a triangle: punch beats throw beats kick beats punch
  • Keep on bouncing back and forth until either a defend happens or damage is taken

Put a card in your stance

  • Card goes face up on the table (all players can see it)
  • But enables more options
